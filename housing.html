416 Beacon Street

The Delta Tau Delta fraternity house is a beautiful brownstone located in Boston's Back Bay at 416 Beacon Street. The great location provides easy access to the Prudential Center, Boston Commons, Fenway Park, Kenmore and Hynes Convention Center T stops, and MIT.

A view of our roofdeck at sunset.

Amenities

Housing comes with complete access to all the amenities of 416 Beacon Street. We have a TV room with a 65-inch TV, pool table, and piano. There is a ping pong table and bar downstairs. We have a fully equipped kitchen and laundry room. Rooms are furnished with couches, mattresses, desks, dressers, and closet space. All rooms have high speed internet and the option of DirecTV. Roofdeck equipped with tables, chairs, couches, a hammock, and a grill.

A view of the TV room, including a 65-inch TV, pool table, and piano.

House Rules

Move in day is Saturday May 23 and move out day is Friday August 21. These dates can be moved by a day or two, given special circumstances. Summer renters are allowed to have guests over, within reason. Renters are responsible for the actions of their guests. Renters are responsible for the cleanliness of their own rooms.

Common areas, kitchen, and bathrooms will be cleaned by brothers on a nightly basis. Additional details will be provided by the summer house manager on move in day.

The spacious front entrance to the Delts house and a view of the front stairs.

Pricing

Rental rates for summer 2015 are:

  • Quint: $1650
  • Quad: $1750
  • Triple: $1850
  • Double: $2050

Prices are per-person and are for the entire summer, Saturday May 23 - Friday August 21. Move in and move out dates can be flexible in special circumstances at the discretion of the summer house manager. Prices cannot be pro-rated for a shorter stay.

A security deposit is required to guarantee housing.

The rental rate includes utilities and full access to all amenities described above.

A fully furnished quint with lofted beds, couches, desks, and closet space.

Parking

We have a 6-car garage as well as 2 street spots available for rent. Spaces can be rented on a first-come first-serve basis for $300.
