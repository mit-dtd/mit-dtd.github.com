Rush is just around the corner, and we couldn't be more excited to meet the future brothers of Delta Tau Delta. We've got a great rush schedule planned, including jet skiing, indoor skydiving, and paintball.

We are looking for brothers who are smart, hard-working, confident, and driven. In addition to these things, we are looking for brothers who want more out of MIT than just a great education.

Delts offers its brothers important friendships, a bustling social life, and a true chance to grow as a person. Delts enjoy one of the closest-knit brotherhoods known to any college campus, and any Delt will tell you how much he values the friendships and connections he's made here.

If you have any questions about Delta Tau Delta or about rush, please contact me at:

andrew16@mit.edu or (630) 800-8417.

If you think you may be a good fit, we would love to see you at our rush events. My brothers and I look forward to meeting you.

Andrew Hochstadt, Rush Chairman
