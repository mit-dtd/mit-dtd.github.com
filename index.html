Academics Delta Tau Delta has a record for some of the most talented brothers in academics.

Coursework is a priority to us.

Here at Delta Tau Delta, we recognize that we’re here at MIT to, first and foremost, learn and grow. Our academics are of the highest priority and we’re proud to harbor highly talented and intelligent brothers who go on and become the greatest they can be. With brothers who span across 12 different majors, Delta Tau Delta is a diverse group of intellectuals who know how to take their work seriously.


Course Libraries

Over the years, brothers, and now alumni, of Delta Tau Delta have always supported each other in their coursework. A library of course content, notes, and texts have been accumulated over the decades of the brotherhood. These allow every brother a glimpse into how past brothers have emerged successful at their time here at MIT, particularly with classes. Our Course Library also contains the schedules of past brothers and suggestions by graduated seniors who would like to pass on their own personal skills to all present and future brothers.


Academic tutors

Brothers who know their course materials inside and out, are elected to make sure every brother comprehends, to the fullest extent, their coursework. This ensures that each brother is held to the highest standards for their grades and helps them in areas where they might need assistance.

Social We boast a lively social program with large parties, sorority mixers, cocktail nights, and a formal.

Sick Bashes

These are our largest parties, they attract students from MIT, BU, Northeastern, Wellesley, Tufts, and several others. Each party has a theme that makes for a great time. Beach party, Toga, Grog, Graveyard, and Goldfish are some of the best parties in Boston. Don’t just take our word for it. Ask around and you’ll hear stories. These parties are where true college memories are created. Dress to theme and don’t miss out.


Cocktail Parties

This is where we get fancy. Once in awhile we host an invite-only cocktail party on our beautiful roof deck. It’s more relaxing and quaint than the bigger parties, but debatably more fun. We hire a live Berklee jazz band to truly set the mood. Shirt and tie for mandatory for guys. Dresses mandatory for girls.


Mixers

We commonly mix with sororities. Delts have great relations with sororities from MIT and other schools throughout Boston. To keep these relations up, we host a variety of sorority mixers each semester, each with a different feel, set-up, and theme. We use mixers to get to know the girls more personally and to blow off stress from tough academic weeks.


Formal

Formal is another place where we get fancy. Once a semester, we spare no expense on Formal. Examples of past Formals are a Harbor Cruise, dinner at the Top of the Hub, Blue Man Group, comedy club show, and a private dinner in the North End. Usually the night continues with ice-skating on frog pond or dessert at Cheesecake Factory.

Sports Delts Athletics have been notorious for being team-leading, hard-working.

Varsity

We have a diverse group of athletes who excel at their respective sports. To name a few, we have brothers engaged in track, swimming, diving, crew, baseball, soccer, football, fencing, and lacrosse. Several brothers have even been team leader in their sports. Whatever the activity may be, Delts has been known to put in the most rigorous efforts to be the best they can be.


Intramural

Intramural sports are also a big part of Delts athletics. Our favorites are ultimate Frisbee, indoor soccer, bowling, softball, and football. Our team almost always signs up for league A participation, which is the most competitive league possible.
